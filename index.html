Upload & Extract Invoice
Photograph each page of the vendor invoice and select all pages at once when uploading. Tap Extract All with Claude — the app reads every style code, color, size, quantity, and wholesale cost automatically, and generates a product name for every style.
Product Names
Tap the circular arrow (↻) next to any name for a new AI-generated one, as many times as you like. Or tap the name and type your own. Whatever is in the field at push time goes to Shopify.
Setting Prices
Use Apply All for the same markup everywhere (50% or $20), or set Markup / Retail per card. Retail calculates automatically and rounds up to the next 50¢. Wholesale cost is visible only to you.
Push to Shopify
The review screen shows every product before it's sent. Products are immediately live after each push — no draft step, no undo from the app. Every variant gets a barcode (its SKU) so it can be scanned in Floor Count and printed on hang tags.
Duplicate Warning
If a style already exists in Shopify you must decide: Add Inventory (restock), Push as New (separate listing), or Skip. The app never modifies an existing product without your decision.

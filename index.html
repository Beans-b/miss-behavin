Upload & Extract Invoice
Photograph each page of the vendor invoice and select all pages at once when uploading — no need to do them one at a time.
Tap Extract All with Claude. The app reads every style code, color, size, quantity, and wholesale cost automatically.
A product name is auto-generated for every style. You don't need to do anything — they're ready to go.
Product Names
Each style card has a small circular arrow button (↻) next to the name. Tap it to get a new AI-generated name. Tap as many times as you like until you find one you want.
You can also tap the name field directly and type your own. Whatever is in the field at the time of push is what goes into Shopify.
Setting Prices
Use Apply All at the top to set the same markup across every style at once. Enter a percentage like 50% or a flat dollar amount like $20.
Or set prices individually on each card using the Markup and Retail fields. The retail price calculates automatically from your markup.
Wholesale cost is visible only to you. Staff see retail prices only.
Push to Shopify
Before anything is sent, a review screen shows every product that will be created — name, color, sizes, price, and inventory quantities. Check everything carefully here.
When you're ready, tap Push to Shopify.
Once pushed, products are immediately live on the website and available at POS. There is no draft step and no undo from the app. Review carefully before pushing.
Duplicate Warning
If a style card turns red on the review screen, that style code already exists as a product in Shopify. You must make a decision before the push can continue.
Add Inventory — adds the new quantities to the existing product. Use this for restocks.
Push as New — creates a second separate listing in Shopify.
Skip — excludes this style from the push. Handle it manually in Shopify.
The app will never modify an existing product automatically. Every duplicate requires your decision first.

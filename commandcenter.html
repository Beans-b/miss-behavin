Hard guardrails

Violating the top group breaks POS. Non-negotiable.

Never change

  • Product titles (tied to in-store POS tags)
  • Handles, SKUs, barcodes, variant IDs
  • Prices, tags, inventory, locations
  • theme.liquid lines 39–40 on the live theme
  • Rolla's + Gorjana content until the stocking decision

Safe to do

  • Search-engine-listing fields (page title + meta)
  • Product category, descriptions, image alt + filename
  • Fill-empty only — never overwrite existing SEO
  • Theme work on the draft; publish only on approval
  • Hidden blog drafts — never auto-publish

Writing rules

  • The word "literally" — cut it entirely
  • Minimal hyphens in anything published
  • Fabricated metrics or citations — placeholders instead
  • Plain confident language, lead with the point
  • Every post: ≥1 concrete number + ≥1 quotable staff line
